# Life Cycle of the Cocoon Swarm

The journey from a single line of intent to a full-scale AI workforce follows the natural metamorphosis of the butterfly.

## 1. Egg (Spin Phase)
`cocoon spin --name "my_swarm"`
- Initializes the `cocoon_manifest.json` (The DNA).
- Sets budget guardrails and project scope.

## 2. Larva (Hatch Phase)
`cocoon hatch --task "Build a SaaS"`
- Decomposes intent into specialized workers (Larvae).
- Each sub-task is assigned to a specific expert (Coder, Scraper, etc.).

## 3. Pupa (Silk Phase)
`cocoon silk`
- Parallel autonomous execution.
- Inter-agent coordination via the Hive (Rust) runtime.

## 4. Imago (Emerge Phase)
`cocoon emerge`
- Validation and result harvesting.
- The project "takes flight" as a complete product.
